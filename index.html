All kinds of marmosets
The pygmy marmoset is notable for being the smallest monkey and one of the smallest primates in the world.
Common marmosets employ a number of vocal and visual communications.
The black-tufted marmoset is characterized by black tufts of hair around their ears.
Wied's marmosets are matriarchal, and only the dominant female is allowed to mate.
